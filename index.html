Roadmap for a Veterans Tech Transition

Welcome Veterans

The Veteran Tech Transition Hub is your launchpad into the tech industry following your time in service. We understand transitioning from military life into the civilian workforce can be difficult, and the VTTH is here to help. Whether you're planning to separate or are already separated, this site offers guidance, resources, and encouragement tailored to your journey.

Step-by-Step Roadmap for Veterans looking to transition into tech

  1. Assess your interests and strengths

    Do you enjoy working with computers? Are you okay with a desk job? If yes, proceed to Step 2.

  2. Explore tech career paths

    3. Explore which tech career interests you (cybersecurity, networking, software development). If you're unsure, please see our resources to take a personality test that suggests careers based on your interests.

    Once you've decided which career path to follow, proceed to Step 3.

  3. Enroll in the school of your choice

    4. Enroll in the right school or program to receive the diploma or certificate(s) you may need to enter said field. Our resources section provides veteran friendly schools that offer tech certifications and degree plans.

    Once you've received your certificate(s) or diploma, proceed to Step 4.

  4. Build your resume

    5. See our resources tab for free veteran friendly resume building services. Once your resume is polished, proceed to Step 5.

  5. Network with mentors and veteran-friendly employers.

    6. Networking to land the job of your choice can be daunting, but our resource tab provides websites that help veterans network. If you have networked and landed a job, please proceed to the final step.

  6. Congrats! You've made it!

    7. You've checked every box on the Step-by-Step Roadmap, and should be working for your very first tech job. Stay motivated troops, and know the Veteran Tech Transition Hub is here to assist you along the way into landing a tech job post your military career.
